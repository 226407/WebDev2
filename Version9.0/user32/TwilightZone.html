The Twilight Zone

The Twilight Zone (or Mesopelagic Zone) is the second Oceanic Zone and it extends down to 3,300 feet (1,000 meters). This zone is much darker to the point where plants cannot survive and where the bioluminescence is used by the native animals. It also home to the much of the worlds fish.
